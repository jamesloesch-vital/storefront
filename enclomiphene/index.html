Enclomiphene is prescribed for men experiencing secondary hypogonadism, which is a condition where the testicles do not produce enough testosterone, due to low levels of luteinizing hormone and follicle-stimulating hormone.
Enclomiphene works by blocking estrogen receptors in the brain and stimulating the production of luteinizing hormone and follicle-stimulating hormone, which in turn stimulates testosterone production.
People taking enclomiphene may experience a 1.5x-2.5x increase in testosterone levels, enhanced energy, improved mood, and increased muscle mass within 3-6 months of consistent use. For best results, enclomiphene should be used consistently.
