Frequently Asked Questions

Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT) is a treatment designed to restore and maintain healthy testosterone levels, helping to improve energy, mood, and overall well-being.

TRT works by supplementing or stimulating the body's natural production of testosterone, which can alleviate symptoms associated with low testosterone levels.

Most patients experience enhanced energy, improved mood, and increased muscle mass within 3-6 months of consistent use.

Anastrozole is a medication that helps prevent the conversion of testosterone to estrogen, which can help reduce the risk of estrogen-related side effects.
